About the Artist

Sarah Tanaka is a talented Manga artist with a passion for storytelling and visual art. Her work is characterized by detailed illustrations, dynamic action sequences, and emotionally resonant characters. With several published works and a growing online following, she continues to explore new narrative forms and push the boundaries of the Manga medium.
