About the Artist

I'm Hana Sato, a manga artist dedicated to crafting captivating stories through sequential art. With a lifelong passion for drawing and a deep appreciation for Japanese culture, I strive to create artwork that resonates emotionally and visually with readers. My style blends traditional techniques with modern digital tools, resulting in detailed and dynamic illustrations. Whether it's the intensity of a battle scene or the subtlety of character expressions, I aim to bring each panel to life.
